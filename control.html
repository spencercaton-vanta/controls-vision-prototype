How the organization meets the requirements above. The substance of the control.
Vanta maintains a written Access Control Policy that defines the lifecycle of access: request, approval, provisioning, role modification, quarterly review, and revocation on role change or termination. Access requests are recorded as Jira tickets in the SEC project, approved by the system owner of record, and provisioned within defined SLAs (4 business hours standard, 1 hour emergency). The policy is reviewed annually by the CISO and acknowledged by all personnel during onboarding via the HR Onboarding Workflow.
