Edit
A written Access Control Policy defines the lifecycle of access: request, approval, provisioning, role modification, quarterly review, and revocation on role change or termination. Requests are Jira tickets in the SEC project, approved by the system owner of record, and provisioned within SLAs (4h standard, 1h emergency). The policy is reviewed annually by the CISO and acknowledged at hire via the HR Onboarding Workflow.
